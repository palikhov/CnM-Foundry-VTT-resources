Hey automation lovers!
Showcasing Chaos Bolt Macro for 5e using a combination of Midi-Qol. This bad boy will automatically roll the 2d8, give you a list of elements to choose from. Then if they match, it'll will give you another list of new targets to pick from.
The cycle continues until you run out of targets!
By popular demand, here it is. It's not tested in heavy game play, but have fun with it.

Module Requirements
Midi-qol
